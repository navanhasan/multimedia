Apples VS Bananas

Apples

Apples are red fruits that taste very well and juicy. It has a crunchy surface, but a delicious core. It is very rich in fiber, and has many healthy benefits. Some of the major benefits include increasing immunity system or getting rid of viruses from the body. There are more than 1,000 different types of apples because of cross-breeding and agriculture.

Bananas

Bananas are yellow fruits that have a peel on the outside and have light berry on the inside. They contain a lot of potassium, vitamins, and also help with body structures. They are known as plaintains and can only be grown by herb and not by seed.
